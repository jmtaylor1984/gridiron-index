Method

How the library is built — and what we won't pretend.

Sourcing

Entries are gathered by searching YouTube for open-access football coaching instruction tied to a specific position. We prioritize national coaching organizations (AFCA, Glazier Clinics), recognized coaching educators (MatchQuarters / Cody Alexander, All Eyes DB Camp / Chad Wilson), USA Football, and named college or NFL coaches. Speaker credentials are confirmed from the video title, description, or on-screen title cards wherever possible.

Why we don't rank by rating

YouTube removed public dislike counts in November 2021, and aggregate review data for individual videos is not reliably available to outside tools. Instead of pretending to know what's "highest rated," we filter by four working criteria:

  • Reputable source — national associations, established clinics, or recognized educator channels.
  • Named, credentialed speaker — identified in title, description, or on-screen, with a verifiable coaching role.
  • Direct position relevance — instruction tied to a specific position, not general football commentary.
  • Coaching value — drill specificity, technique breakdown, or scheme detail useful to a position coach.

TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook clips are excluded by design. Videos from unclear or anonymous sources are excluded unless uniquely valuable for a position not otherwise well covered.
